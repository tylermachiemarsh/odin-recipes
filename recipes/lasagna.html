Lasagna

Lasagna? Yes please!

Once just an Italian staple, lasagna can now be found in homes all over the world.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 LB Ground Beef
  • 1 LB Hot Breakfast Sausage
  • 2 Cloves Garlic-Minced
  • 2 Cans Whole Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 TBSP Driend Parsley
  • 2 TBSP Dried Basil
  • 2.5 TSP Salt
  • 3 C Lowfat Cottage Cheese
  • 2 Whole Beaten Eggs
  • 1/2 C Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 TBSP Dried Parsley
  • 1 TBSP Olive Oil
  • 1 Package Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 LB Sliced Mozzarella Cheese
